Pizza

Description

A leavened wheat-based dough topped with chicken, veggies, tomato sauce and cheese

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast Boneless (cut in cubes)
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper Powder
  • Cheeses

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C.
  2. In a bowl add chicken cubes, oil, salt and black pepper and marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. Spread pizza topping on pizza base. Place capsicum, onion, tomato, olives and marinated chicken. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top to cover the vegetables and chicken.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes or until cheese melts and pizza bases turns light brown.
